Digitization refers to converting physical, paper-based documents (like PDFs, images, XPS files) into structured, machine-readable data using AI-driven technologies.

By leveraging digitization, organizations can transform document-heavy processes into automated, AI-powered workflows, resulting in operational efficiency, cost savings, and faster revenue cycle management.

This transformation involves capturing data—like text, images, audio, or video—and representing it as digital files, such as PDFs, spreadsheets, or multimedia files.

  • Improved Accessibility: Digital data can be accessed anytime, anywhere.
  • Better Storage: Reduces physical storage needs and ensures long-term preservation.
  • Faster Processing: Speeds up workflows and data retrieval.
  • Enhanced Security: Digital formats allow encryption and controlled access.

In today’s data-driven world, businesses and organizations constantly interact with a vast array of documents such as PDFs, images, and XPS files.
